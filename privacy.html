Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

Thank you for choosing Attendance Pro. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information when you use our attendance monitoring system.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following data:

  • Full Name, ID Number, and contact details
  • Attendance logs (time-in, time-out, absences, tardiness)
  • Leave requests and approval history
  • Role and access level (Admin, Employee, Student, Teacher, etc.)
  • User authentication data (passwords, identifiers, biometrics if enabled)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for:

  • Tracking attendance accurately
  • Generating reports and analytics
  • Providing schedule and shift management
  • Sending notifications and alerts
  • Maintaining account security

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement strict security measures including encryption, secure servers, role-based access, and optional biometric authentication to ensure only authorized users can access sensitive data.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal data to third parties. Data is only shared:

  • With your employer or school administrator
  • When required by law, court requests, or security investigations

5. Data Retention

We keep attendance records as long as necessary for operations or legal compliance. Users may request deletion where applicable.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Update incorrect information
  • Request data deletion (where applicable)
  • Restrict processing in certain situations

7. Cookies & Tracking

Our platform may use cookies to improve user experience, but does not track unnecessary personal data.

8. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
